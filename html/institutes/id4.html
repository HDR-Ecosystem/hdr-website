About

The Institute for Data-Driven Dynamical Design (ID4), based at the Colorado School of Mines, is an interdisciplinary research institute dedicated to transforming the design cycle of molecules, materials, and structures through the integration of human expertise and machine intelligence. By developing novel approaches to navigating complex design spaces, tailoring artificial intelligence for domain-specific simulations, and enabling knowledge distillation strategies, ID4 accelerates the discovery of new functional mechanisms across scales ranging from the atomic to the structural.

Mission

“From chemistry to civil engineering, we seek to create platforms that accelerate the discovery of new mechanisms and dynamics through the complementary union of human and machine intelligence. We aspire to create the next generation of leaders in this transformative approach and to cultivate a vibrant, inclusive community committed to data-driven approaches.”

Vision

“By revolutionizing the design cycle of molecules, materials, and structures, ID4 aims to reveal new mechanisms for fabrication and function across length scales.”
