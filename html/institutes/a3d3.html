About

Based at the University of Washington in collaboration with leading institutions nationwide, the Accelerated, Agile, and Automated Science from Data (A3D3) Institute is funded by the NSF HDR program to pioneer real-time artificial intelligence at scale for scientific discovery. A3D3 targets major data-rich frontiers in high-energy physics, multi-messenger astrophysics, and systems neuroscience, and co-develops specialized AI algorithms alongside advanced hardware (GPUs, FPGAs) to process massive, complex datasets in real time. Through its distributed, multidisciplinary team, A3D3 delivers customized AI solutions, builds institutional knowledge, and accelerates science across domains by integrating method, infrastructure, and scientific application.

Mission

“To lead a paradigm shift in applying real-time AI at scale in order to advance scientific knowledge and accelerate discovery.”

Vision

“A scientific future where real-time AI transforms the way knowledge is generated, enabling breakthroughs across disciplines”
