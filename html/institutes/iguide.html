About

Based at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, the Institute for Geospatial Understanding through an Integrative Discovery Environment (I-GUIDE) advances the frontiers of data-intensive geospatial science by integrating geospatial artificial intelligence, cyberGIS, and high-performance computing. I-GUIDE constructs an open, integrative discovery environment that empowers convergence research on socio-environmental systems. I-GUIDE is shaping a more inclusive, data-driven geospatial research ecosystem that enhances societal decision-making and environmental stewardship.

Mission

"Advance convergence and geospatial sciences for holistic sustainability solutions"

Vision

""Drive digital discovery and innovation by harnessing the geospatial data revolution"
